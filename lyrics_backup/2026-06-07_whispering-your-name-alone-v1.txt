[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers glowing when you lean into the dark
A silent kind of thunder striking right across my heart
Your shadow in the doorway is a promise in the room
And I just want to trace the way you breathe against the moon

[Verse 1]
The wooden floors are creaking with a secret we can keep
A solitary lantern casting light across our feet
There is a sweet magnetic pull that drags me to your skin
A beautiful unraveling where you and I begin

[Pre-Chorus]
The evening grows heavy and the air is turning slow
You give me a single look and let the quiet fire grow
I am holding back the rush but I am crumbling inside
There is nowhere left to run and there is nowhere left to hide

[Chorus]
Oh I am sinking in the deep end of your midnight eyes
Lost inside the gentle way you wear the starry skies
Every breath you take is calling out my lonely soul
You are the only missing piece that ever made me whole

[Post-Chorus]
Just lay your head against my chest and let the shadows fall
Whispering your name alone, I answer to your call

[Verse 2]
The scent of fallen rain is lingering across your hair
I reach across the table just to know that you are there
A sudden brush of fingers sends a shiver down my spine
And everything is fading as our beating hearts align

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The evening grows heavy and the air is turning slow
You give me a single look and let the quiet fire grow
I am holding back the rush but I am crumbling inside
There is nowhere left to run and there is nowhere left to hide

[Chorus]
Oh I am sinking in the deep end of your midnight eyes
Lost inside the gentle way you wear the starry skies
Every breath you take is calling out my lonely soul
You are the only missing piece that ever made me whole

[Post-Chorus 2]
Just lay your head against my chest and let the shadows fall
Whispering your name alone, I answer to your call

[Bridge]
The clock is ticking softly as the morning tries to break
I want to memorize the little movements that you make
You pull me even closer as I finally concede
A gentle kind of magic when you whisper to Saeed

[Spoken]
(Just stay right here in the quiet)
(Let the morning wait for us)

[Final Chorus]
Oh I am sinking in the deep end of your midnight eyes
Lost inside the gentle way you wear the starry skies
Every breath you take is calling out my lonely soul
You are the only missing piece that ever made me whole
